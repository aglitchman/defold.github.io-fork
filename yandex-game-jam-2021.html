Theme: Target

The theme for this game jam is "Target". You must incorporate some aspect of the theme into your game, and it will be part of the judging criteria for the "Defold's Choice" category (see below). Remember that the word target can mean much more than the literal target of an arrow or a gun. It could just as well be a target number or score or some other goal or objective you need to achieve to complete a level.

Rules

  • You must create an original game for the game jam and you must develop it during the game jam.
  • You must incorporate some aspect of the theme in the game.
  • You must use the Defold game engine when you create your game.
  • You are allowed to use existing tools, frameworks and libraries when creating the game.
  • You are allowed to use free or paid graphics, sounds and music that you already own.
  • You must integrate and use the Yandex.Games SDK in the game.
  • You can create the game on your own or as part of a team.
